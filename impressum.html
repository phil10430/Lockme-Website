Privacy Policy

Personal information

StimMe is made with your privacy in mind, so no personally identifiable information about you is collected. In other words, StimMe does not collect information such as your name, address, phone number, email address, or geographic location.

Microphone usage

Within the mode "Punish" you can activate the "Noise-Mode", where a punishment is triggered by exceeding a certain noise level. To detect the noise level the microphone in your device is activated. However StimMe does not store the recorded audio.

Changes

In case there are any privacy policy changes this page will be updated.
